Important Rules for Online Exam
  1. Device and Internet: Ensure your device is fully charged and connected to a stable, high-speed internet connection.
  2. Webcam and Microphone: Keep your webcam and mic turned on during the exam for monitoring.
  3. No Unauthorized Tabs or Devices: Don't use phones, smartwatches, or open other browser tabs.
  4. Stay in Frame: Keep your face clearly visible throughout the exam.
  5. Quiet Environment: Choose a distraction-free area for your exam.
  6. Individual Effort: The exam must be attempted individually.
  7. Behavior Monitoring: Your webcam and mic activity will be monitored by AI proctoring.
  8. Rule Violations: Misconduct may lead to immediate termination and disciplinary action.
